Course manager

Welcome to our Student Manager application! Our system allows you to manage courses and teachers. You can add new courses, assign teachers to courses, and enroll students in individual classes. To add a new course, use the "Add Course" form in the course list. Provide the course name and select the appropriate teacher from the available options. If you want to see a list of all courses, students and teachers, go to the "Courses", "Teachers" or "Students" sections. Feel free to use our system, and we wish you successful academic management!
